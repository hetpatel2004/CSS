Swaminarayan — Saints (Sants / Nand Saints)

Who are the Saints?

When Swaminarayan Bhagwan established the Sampraday, He ordained nearly 3000 ascetic saints — collectively known as the “Nand Saints.” :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} These saints came from diverse backgrounds — scholars, simple farmers, young bachelors, people with families — but upon meeting Swaminarayan Bhagwan, they renounced material life and dedicated themselves wholly to spiritual service. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Vows (Five-fold Discipline)

All Nand Saints adhered strictly to five core vows (Panch-Vartman):

  • Nishkam: Complete abstinence from women. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
  • Nirlobh: Free from all wealth and greed. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Nirman: Dispose of ego and pride; humbly surrender to God. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Niswad: Transcend desire for taste (food/drink). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
  • Ni­s-sneh: Detach from material relationships and possessions. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Role & Contributions of the Saints
  • They travelled across remote villages to spread satsang and introduce devotees to the teachings of Swaminarayan Bhagwan. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}
  • They authored scriptures, composed kirtans, and preserved the spiritual heritage for future generations. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
  • They established and maintained temples, making devotional worship accessible to common people throughout regions. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}
  • Through their steadfast devotion and sacrifices, they laid the foundations upon which the Swaminarayan Sampraday grew and flourished. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

Some Prominent Saints

The tradition remembers many saints by name. Some of the important ones include:

  • Brahmanand Swami
  • Gunatitanand Swami
  • Muktanand Swami
  • Nishkulanand Swami
  • Gopalanand Swami

These names appear among lists of holy saints preserved in the sampraday. :contentReference[oaicite:17]{index=17}

Legacy of the Saints

Thanks to their renunciation, penance, and tireless efforts, the sampraday was able to reach remote corners — spreading devotion, establishing temples, imparting scriptural wisdom, and uplifting social standards. Their legacy continues to inspire devotees even today. :contentReference[oaicite:18]{index=18}
